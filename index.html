Insulin Prescribing Calculator

Welcome to the insulin prescribing calculator!
Step 1: Use the leftmost dropdown to select the brand, form, and concentration of insulin desired.
Step 2: Use the dropdown in the middle to select the desired supply.
Step 3: Use the text box on the right to enter the desired units per day, and then press tab (pressing enter will reset the calculator).
NOTE 1: Information will appear as soon as a change is made to the default values below!
NOTE 2: The two numbers outputted by the program differ, because insulin may not be orderable in the exact size required by a patient! Thus, the "to order" number rounds the amount of insulin required up to nearest orderable amount.
